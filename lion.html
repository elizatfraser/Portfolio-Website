Brief
Create a 90 page document that would contain every beverage that Lion holds within their Australia/New Zealand portfolio

Deliverables
Exploratory research, icon design, infographics, typography layout

Role
Graphic Designer

Design tools
Adobe InDesign, Illustrator and Photoshop

Lion is New Zealand’s leading beverage manufacturer and distributor, and needed a document that contained information on every beverage in their portfolio.

I was asked by one of the directors to create a book that would give information on each beverage, it’s format that we sell it in, tasting notes, dietary notes, region it is made in and price bracket.

This was a solo design project which needed to be created in time around my existing workflow.

In order to complete this task I needed to be highly organised with my time, so that I could deliver this project on schedule.

I created a spreadsheet that housed all of the information and the director could update as they needed, and I worked off for the content of the book.

I developed a design system of icons and infographics to help break up the content heavy pages, and researched alternative ways to present information so that the finished product would be easy to navigate and understand for the user.

The finished book was successful and they were able to implement the usage of it throughout the business. It was delivered on schedule and within the expected budget. The feedback from the director was incredibly positive as I was able to bring his idea to life.
