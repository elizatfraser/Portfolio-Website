Brief
Create point of sale material to support the launch of Gordon’s Pink Gin in New Zealand

Deliverables
Posters, menu’s, welcome cards, social media campaigns and ad hoc design requests

Role
Graphic Designer

Design tools
Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop and After Effects

With the launch of Gordon’s Pink Gin in New Zealand, my role was to develop the below the line campaign assets for use in restaurants, bars and hotels.

The challenge was to create assets that suited the style of each venue, while also maintaining the look and feel of the above the line campaign launch. Brand guidelines for the launch were created and we were provided with a design system of logos and photography.

I approached this project with the needs of the venue at the forefront, their goal was to promote a new product which would increase interest and drive sales.

Using both print and digital platforms I designed collateral that answered their needs by giving them them access to a range of assets, each designed specifically with their venue in mind.

Avoiding a “one size fits all” approach, I took the time to ensure each request was met with consideration and attention to detail.

The final result was a successful and cohesive national launch, with Gordon’s Pink becoming one of the leading new product releases of 2018.
