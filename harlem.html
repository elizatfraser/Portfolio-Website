Harlem

Brief
Create a brand identity for an activation by Harlem City Council

Role
Graphic Designer

Deliverables
Exploratory research, infographics, typography layout

Design tools
Adobe InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop and After Effects

Harlem City Council wanted to launch an activation to increase awareness around the rich diversity of their neighbourhood.

The challenge was to design the identity for this activation, using typography and infographics, without photography.

I really enjoyed the challenge of avoiding the use of photography, as I feel it can often detract from a simple and clean design. I was able to make the content the hero and focused on the message with clever use of typography.

The finished design answered the brief by presenting a clean and fresh image for Harlem. The energy of the area was reflected in the variation of size in content and a confident colour palette.

This brief taught me the value of type and ways to use it as a key design feature, rather than secondary to photography.
