Meet Eliza

Hi, I'm a London based UX/UI designer with a background in graphic design.

I’ve always focused on delivering thoughtful solutions for my clients, and this user-centric philosophy is what led me to user experience design.

My background in both in-house and agency design teams has taught me how to balance both client and business needs while developing a pixel-perfect approach to all my concepts.

I’m now enjoying applying the same principles to designing intuitive digital solutions that consider the experience of their users first.

For more information on past and present projects that I am involved with, please get in touch.
